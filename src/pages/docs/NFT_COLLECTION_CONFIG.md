## Combo System

### Minor Combos (2-5 cards)
- Same species collection
- Color matching
- Habitat grouping

### Major Combos (6-10 cards)
- Complete category
- Ecosystem balance
- Spirit animal gathering

### Ultimate Combos
- **Complete Farm** (Chickens + Pigeons + Goats)
- **Wild Complete** (All wild animals)
- **Spirits United** (All legendary/spirit cards)
- **The Grand Ecosystem** (All 112 cards)

## Airdrop Schedule

### Daily Airdrops
- 1 Common card guaranteed
- Small chance of Rare

### Weekly Airdrops
- 1 Rare card guaranteed
- Small chance of Epic

### Monthly Airdrops
- 1 Epic card guaranteed
- Small chance of Legendary

### Special Event Airdrops
- Project milestone completion
- Community challenges
- Seasonal events
- Legendary card opportunities

### Contribution Rewards
- $10+ contribution = Bronze tier airdrop (1 Rare)
- $25+ contribution = Silver tier airdrop (1 Epic)
- $50+ contribution = Gold tier airdrop (1 Legendary)
- $100+ contribution = Diamond tier airdrop (2 Legendaries)
- $500+ contribution = Platinum tier airdrop (Mythic chance)

## Visual Design Notes

### Card Layout (from wireframe)
- **Header:** Collection name + Card title
- **Main Image:** 240x180px illustration area
- **Category Badge:** Top left corner
- **Rarity Star:** Top right corner
- **Edition Number:** Below image
- **Attributes:** Type, Rarity, Habitat, Trait
- **Combo Indicators:** Active combo bonuses
- **Footer:** Mint date, Project source

### Color Coding (for final design)
- Common: Gray/Silver
- Rare: Blue
- Epic: Purple
- Legendary: Gold
- Mythic: Holographic/Rainbow
